The Shawshank Redemption is a drama film directed by Frank Darabont, based on the Stephen King novella Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption. The film tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence. Over the years, Andy befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and finds a sense of hope and redemption within the prison walls.
